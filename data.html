    Historical Inflation Rates: 1914-2023

    The table displays historical inflation rates with annual figures from 1914 to the present. The inflation rates are calculated using the Consumer Price Index, which is published monthly by the Bureau of Labor Statistics (BLS) of the U.S. Department of Labor. The latest BLS data, covering up to March 2023, was released on April 12, 2023. The next inflation update is scheduled for May 10, 2023, and will provide historical inflation rates up to April 2023.

    For visual charts and tables that focus on most recent inflation data, please refer to current inflation rates. For calculating the cumulative rate of inflation between specific dates, please refer to the US Inflation Calculator.
